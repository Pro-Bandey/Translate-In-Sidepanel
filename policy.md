# Privacy Policy for [Extension Name]

**Effective Date:** March 17, 2026

## 1. Information Collection and Use
[Extension Name] is a translation tool that allows users to translate selected text. 
To provide this service, the extension transmits the text you select to third-party translation providers (such as Google Translate, Bing Microsoft Translator, or DeepL).

## 2. Data Transmission
- **No Local Storage:** We do not store, log, or save your selected text or translation history on our own servers.
- **Third-Party Services:** The text is sent via a secure (HTTPS) connection to the translation API you have selected in the extension settings. 
- **Processing:** This data is processed in real-time by the third-party provider to return a translation. 

## 3. Data We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect or store:
- Personally Identifiable Information (name, email, etc.)
- Browsing history or URLs of the websites you visit
- Usage analytics or tracking data

## 4. Third-Party Policies
Because data is sent to external APIs, we encourage you to review their respective privacy policies:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com)
- [Microsoft Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com)
- [DeepL Privacy Policy](https://www.deepl.com)
